Privacy Policy

Data Protection Overview

Our Commitment

I take the protection of your personal data seriously and handle your personal information in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This policy explains what personal data I collect when you visit this website and how it’s used.

Who is responsible for data collection?

Data processing on this website is carried out by the website operator. Contact details can be found in the Legal Notice section of this website.

What data do I collect?

Data you provide directly:

  • Contact information when you use the contact form or scheduling system
  • Any information you voluntarily provide when contacting me

Data collected automatically:

  • Basic usage analytics through Google Analytics (anonymized)
  • Browser information and device data (processed by GitHub Pages hosting)

Your Rights

Regarding data I directly control (contact form responses, meeting scheduling):

  • Request information about your stored personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

For data processed by third-party services (Google Analytics, GitHub hosting, Formspree, Calendly), please contact those services directly or refer to their individual privacy policies.

Contact me using the details in the Legal Notice for any inquiries about data I directly handle.

Detailed Information

Website Hosting

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may process technical data (IP addresses, browser information) as part of their hosting service. See GitHub’s Privacy Statement for details.

Google Analytics

I use Google Analytics to understand website usage patterns. This service:

  • Collects anonymized usage data
  • Uses cookies to track visitor behavior
  • Helps me improve the website experience

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Legal basis: Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) for website optimization.

Contact Forms (Formspree)

When you use the contact form, your data is processed by Formspree. This includes:

  • Your name and email address
  • Any message content you provide
  • Processing for the purpose of responding to your inquiry

Legal basis: Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) when you submit the form.

Data is retained until the purpose of processing is fulfilled or you request deletion.

Calendly Integration

If you use the embedded scheduling system, Calendly processes:

  • Your name and contact information
  • Meeting preferences and scheduling data
  • Communication related to scheduled meetings

See Calendly’s Privacy Policy for details about their data handling.

Legal basis: Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) when you use the scheduling service.

Cookies

This website uses cookies for:

  • Essential website functionality
  • Google Analytics tracking (with your consent)
  • Calendly scheduling functionality

You can configure your browser to block cookies, though this may limit website functionality.

SSL Encryption

This website uses SSL/TLS encryption to protect data transmission. You can identify an encrypted connection by the “https://” in the address bar and the lock symbol in your browser.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in legal requirements or website functionality. Please review this page periodically for updates.

Contact for Data Protection

For questions about data protection or to exercise your rights, contact me using the information provided in the Legal Notice.
